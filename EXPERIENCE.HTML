Experience

Shah Alam Games (SAG)

I once participated in a frisbee tournament, the Shah Alam Games held at UiTM Shah Alam Campus. I represented UiTM Negeri Sembilan Branch in this tournament. I succeeded in becoming one of the main players in this frisbee team after successfully going through the selection process, which also involved players from Pilah Campus and Seremban Campus.

Komander Kesatria Endurance Challenge (KKEC)

In the Komander Kesatria Endurance Challenge, I participated as a runner in the Extreme Outdoor Quest event held at UiTM Arau. In this event, I had to run a distance of 10 kilometers. This event greatly tested both my physical and mental endurance as my competitors were also skilled commanders.

Mawlid Run

In this running event, I managed to secure third place overall. It was the first running event held at UiTM Rembau campus. The running distance was only 2 kilometers, and I clocked a time of 7 minutes for this race.
